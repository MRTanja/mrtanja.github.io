I've started conducting a systematic review and am currently looking for collaborators who are interested in working on it (screening abstracts, extracting data, writing the synthesis). You can read more details in the PROSPERO preregistration [here](https://www.crd.york.ac.uk/PROSPERO/view/CRD420261348311), or in the protocol that is available as a preprint.

The review aims to collate research on multi-shell diffusion MRI in white matter in neurodegenerative and cerebrovascular diseases. We have excluded studies using DTI, regions other than the white matter, and machine learning algorithms that use diffusion MRI metrics as input without directly reporting them. 

If you are interested in hearing more, let me know! There is a large amount of studies to screen (ca. 4,000), but I can say from experience that it doesn't take as long as one might suspect...
